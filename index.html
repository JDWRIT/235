About me:

Hello there! My name is Joshua DeWinter and I am a 2nd year IGME student at RIT. I joined the game development program to continue exploring my love of game and experience creation. I hope continually further my understanding of game development everyday at RIT and prepare myself for all the challenges game production may throw my way in the future. My medium of choice is VR and I have made a game for the Meta Quest 2, however my current knowledge of the game production process does apply to other platforms such as flatscreen and console.
